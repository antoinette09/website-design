Common Mistakes

Confusing Ser and Estar (To Be)

Yo soy cansado. (Suggests "I am a boring person").

Yo estoy cansado. (Means "I am tired")

Misusing Gender Agreement (Adjectives)

La casa es blanco. (Noun is feminine, adjective is masculine).

La casa es blanca. (Noun and adjective both feminine)

Confusion with "Gustar" (Likes)

Me gusto la comida. (Means "I like myself the food").

Me gusta la comida. (Means "I like the food")

Misusing Personal "a"

Visito mi madre. (Missing the personal "a" for a person).

Visito a mi madre. (Correct: "I am visiting my mother")
